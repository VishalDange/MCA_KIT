WELCOME TO KIT's Institute of Management Education & Research,Kolhapur

KIT's Institute of Management Education & Research was established in 1983. It is affiliated to Shivaji University and approved by All India Council For Technical Education, New Delhi. The Institute has over 28 acres beautiful Campus, well furnished lecture rooms and other amenities like syndicate rooms, common rooms, well equipped Library and reading room, computer lab etc.

MCA is three year full time programme affiliated to the Shivaji University. Spaning over six semesters, the course not only includes subjects that give a good grounding in the IT concepts, but also includes management oriented papers like 'Principles of Management',''Accounting and Financial Management', 'Organisational Behaviour' etc, thus preparing the student to be more competent to face the dynamic corporate world.The programme brings out professionals who are not only conversant with technology but also application oriented for various vertical domains.
